Privacy Policy

Kaatu – Fall Detection App

Effective date: 2026-02-08
Last updated: 2026-02-08
On-device processing only No tracking No ads No data sharing

1. Overview

Kaatu is designed to automatically detect when the user falls down. If the user falls, Kaatu can then execute a user configurable sequence of actions in order to alert e.g. loved ones or emergency authorities via SMS or by making an automated audio call. All fall detection functionality works entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers on the Internet.

2. Information We Process (On-Device Only)

To provide fall detection and safety alerts, Kaatu may process the following information locally on your device:

  • Motion sensor data (accelerometer)
  • Activity recognition (to support fall detection accuracy)
  • App settings and preferences (stored locally)
  • Notifications (to display alerts)
  • Optional location data (only if you enable emergency location sharing)

This information is used solely to detect potential falls and provide safety-related alerts.

3. How We Use Information

All processing happens exclusively on your device in order to:

  • Detect potential falls
  • Show notifications on the phone
  • Send user configurable emergency message via SMS
  • (Optional) Share your location during an emergency
  • (Optional) Make a voice call to user configured number as part of the emergency procedure

4. Data Collection and Sharing

Kaatu:

  • Does not collect personal data
  • Does not transmit data to servers
  • Does not use analytics
  • Does not use tracking technologies
  • Does not include advertising SDKs
  • Does not share information with third parties

5. Background Processing

Kaatu may run in the background to continuously monitor for falls. This processing is performed entirely on-device and does not transmit any information externally. Kaatu background processing can be disabled when needed in Kaatu UI.

6. Data Storage

All app data and settings remain stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app deletes all locally stored data associated with Kaatu.

7. Security

Because all processing occurs locally on your device and no data is transmitted externally, your information remains private and under your control.

8. Children’s Privacy

Kaatu is not directed toward children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page and/or within the app.
